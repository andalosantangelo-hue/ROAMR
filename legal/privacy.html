ROAMR Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]. This is a starting template — have it reviewed by a lawyer before launch.

ROAMR ("we") is operated by [LEGAL ENTITY], [ADDRESS]. Questions: privacy@roamr.app.

1. What we collect

  • Account: email address, or phone number, and (for social sign-in) your name and profile photo.
  • Profile & content: display name, bio, interests, and the posts, activities, tribes, comments, listings, and photos you create.
  • Social graph: who you follow, tribes/activities you join, likes and saves.
  • Device & usage: if you consent, privacy-friendly analytics (app opens, feature use). Push notification tokens if you enable notifications.

We do not collect your precise GPS location. Place names you type on posts are content you choose to share. Photos are re-encoded on your device before upload, which removes embedded location/EXIF metadata.

2. How we use it

To provide and operate the app, show you relevant tribes/activities, send notifications you opt into, keep the community safe, and improve the product. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Who processes it

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, and — with consent — Analytics) as our data processor. Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure. See Google's privacy terms for how they handle data on our behalf.

4. Your choices & rights

  • Delete your account anytime in the app (Profile → Account Settings → Delete account). This permanently removes your profile, content, and uploaded photos.
  • Analytics consent can be turned off anytime in Settings.
  • Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR/CCPA), you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing us.

5. Children

ROAMR is not intended for anyone under 13. We ask for date of birth at sign-up and do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Security & retention

Access is restricted by security rules so users can only read their own private data (email, phone, tokens). We retain your data while your account is active and delete it on account deletion.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; we'll post the new date here and, for material changes, notify you in-app.
